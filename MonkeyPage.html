About Monkeys

Monkeys are awesome. They remind me of me and so I like them, and I created this page in their honor.
Monkeys are fascinating creatures that belong to the primate family.They are known for their intelligence,
agility, and playful nature. Monkeys can be found in various parts of the world and come in all shapes and sizes.
